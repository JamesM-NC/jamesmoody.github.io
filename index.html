About Me

Hello! I am a passionate security engineer with nearly two decades of experience designing and implementing secure solutions for global enterprise environments. I’ve had the privilege of working with a Fortune 40 company, where I led efforts in certificate lifecycle management, secrets vault architecture, and hybrid identity infrastructure across cloud and on-prem platforms.

My background includes overseeing over a massive number of active certificates and managing multiple secrets vaults, ensuring high availability, compliance, and cryptographic integrity. I’ve collaborated extensively with cross-functional teams — including legal, engineering, and information security — to establish secure credential workflows and enforce zero-trust authentication strategies.

I’m also a PQC enthusiast, deeply engaged with the evolving standards of Public Key Infrastructure and post-quantum cryptography. I actively follow developments from the CA/B Forum and assist with interpreting RFCs and ballot updates to help ensure my organization maintains compliance with emerging global standards. Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my work and interests. Thank you for visiting!
